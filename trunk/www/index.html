Welcome to the MathRider project.

 

For software developers, MathRider can be thought of as a mathematics-oriented IDE.  For non-developers, MathRider can be thought of as an education-friendly Super Scientific Calculator (SSC) for performing numeric and symbolic computations.

 

MathRider can be downloaded from the "Download" tab shown above.

 

The user's group for MathRider is located at:

http://groups.google.com/group/mathrider-user

 

and the developer's group is located at:

http://groups.google.com/group/mathrider-dev

 

If you would like to join this project, please select the Observer role :-)
