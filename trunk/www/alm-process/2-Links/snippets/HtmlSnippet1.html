Mathematics-oriented websites:

 Cut-The-Knot
This website is capable of providing the foundations for a revolution in mathematics education which complements MathRider's capabilities very well.  Object oriented programmers will be especially interested in reading this talk about applying object oriented principles to mathematics education.
 

 

 MathRider-related educational materials:

 Professor And Pat

 

Learn how a computer works from the ground up.  A hand-held calculator kit which is based on the materials at this site is planned.  The idea is that a person would work through the books at this site, assemble their calculator kit, and then program it in 6502 assembly language.  Very old school and very exciting!

 

Java-based mathematics software: 

MathEclipse

A Java-based Computer Algebra system which has functions for arbitrary-precision integer arithmetic, matrices, vectors, finite sets, derivatives, pattern-matching, term rewriting rules and functional programming.

 

MathRider-related grant activities:

MathRider grant exploration group on Curriki

"Curriki is an online environment created to support the development and free distribution of world-class educational materials to anyone who needs them."  The MathRider group on curriki is for educators who want to collaborate on MathRider-based grant opportunities.
