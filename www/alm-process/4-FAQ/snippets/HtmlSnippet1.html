How do I make expressions which are returned by functions like D() executable?

This is done by placing the expression in an Eval() function: 

 %mathpiper,title=""
 
     f(x) := x^2 + 3*x - 8;
     
     g(x) := Eval(D(x) f(x));
     
     Echo(g(x));
     
     Echo(g(1));
 
 %/mathpiper
 
     %output,preserve="false"
       Result: True
       
       Side Effects:
       2*x+3 
       5
 .   %/output
